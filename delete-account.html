Delete Your rydap Account

At rydap, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal information. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the rydap app.
  2. Go to Profile → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your account deletion request.

If you are unable to access the app, you may also contact us using the email below.

Email:
support@rydap.in

What information will be deleted?

  • Your passenger account.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your saved preferences.
  • Your account information associated with the deleted account.

What information may be retained?

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, regulatory requirements, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, taxation, accounting, or other legal obligations.

Processing time

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 7 business days.
